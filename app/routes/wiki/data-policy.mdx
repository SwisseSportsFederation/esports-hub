# Data Policy
The data collected during the Alpha test phase of the SESF Esports DB, will be deleted once the project reaches the Beta phase. During the Alpha phase there may be data wipes, because of development circumstances.

This data is not used for any other purpose than the SESF Esports DB. If you have any questions, feel free to write a mail to esportsdb@sesf.ch or ask in the SESF Discord channel.
